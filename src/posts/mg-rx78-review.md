# MG RX-78-2 Gundam Ver. 3.0 Review

The RX-78-2 Gundam is the most iconic mobile suit in the franchise, and Bandai has given it numerous model kit releases over the years. The Ver. 3.0 is widely considered the best MG version of the original Gundam.

## Box Contents

- Runner A through L
- Polycap runner
- Beam saber parts
- Marking sticker sheet
- Dry transfer decals
- Instruction manual

## Build Experience

<div style="background: #292929; padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 8px; border-left: 4px solid #0088cb; margin: 1.5rem 0;">
<strong>Build Time:</strong> Approximately 8-10 hours for a straight build
</div>

The construction is well thought out, with excellent part separation that minimizes the need for painting. The inner frame is highly detailed and the armor pieces snap on securely.

## Articulation

The Ver. 3.0 features excellent articulation:

- Full range shoulder movement
- Double-jointed elbows and knees
- Ankle tilts for dynamic poses
- Ab crunch for expressive posing

## Final Verdict

**Rating: 9/10**

This is a must-have for any Gunpla collection. Whether you're a fan of the original series or just appreciate excellent engineering, the MG RX-78-2 Ver. 3.0 delivers on all fronts.

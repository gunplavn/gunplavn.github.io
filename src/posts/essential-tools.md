# Essential Tools for Gunpla Building

Starting your Gunpla journey? Here are the essential tools you'll need to get professional-looking results.

## Tier 1: Absolute Must-Haves

### 1. Side Cutters / Nippers

The most important tool in your arsenal. **Never twist parts off the runner!**

Recommended options:
- **Budget**: Tamiya Sharp Pointed Side Cutter
- **Mid-range**: God Hand SPN-120
- **Premium**: God Hand Ultimate Nipper

### 2. Hobby Knife

For cleaning up nub marks and cutting decals.

- X-Acto #11 blade
- Olfa Art Knife
- Tamiya Modeler's Knife

### 3. Sanding Sticks/Files

Essential for smoothing nub marks:
- 400, 600, 800, 1000 grit progression
- Nail buffer blocks work great too!

## Pro Tips

1. **Buy quality nippers first** - They make the biggest difference
2. **Work in good lighting** - LED desk lamps are ideal
3. **Take your time** - This is a hobby, not a race!

Happy building!
